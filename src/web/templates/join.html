Welcome to the Shelter Database!

Please create your account to enter your shelter into the database. Once you have entered your details and chosen a password you can start entering the data of your shelter immediately into the format. The format contains drop down menus with single or multiple choice, to facilitate your data-entry. Most technical terms are explained in the glossary or illustrated with little technical drawings. Each section also gives the possibility to upload photos. Please try to give as complete information as possible.

Thank you for taking the time to enter new shelters into the database! You are contributing to make the shelter database a valuable tool for other shelter practitioners!

We want to make this a multi-language tool. French and Spanish translation will be provided soon, but we are still looking for volunteers who could translate it into other languages, for example Arabic, Bangla, Hindi, Russian, Urdu, and the major African languages.

If you are interested, or have any questions or recommendations, please don't hesitate to write to ifr-sru@croix-rouge.lu.

The Shelter Database is an initiative of the Benelux Red Cross Societies in collaboration with the IFRC Shelter and Settlements Department. You can see the list of all contributors here.
